About Nourishment

Turning solar micro‑farms into Bronx community assets.

Our Mission

Nourishment installs AI‑supported, solar‑powered micro‑farms across the Bronx so schools, housing sites, and community centers can grow fresh vegetables year‑round.

Project Phases

  • Phase 1: Pilot installations (0–6 months)
  • Phase 2: Scale & governance (6–18 months)
  • Phase 3: Network growth (18–36 months)

Roles

  • Community coordinators
  • Student engineering teams
  • Finance & compliance
  • Distribution partners
